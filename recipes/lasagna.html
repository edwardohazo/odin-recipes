Lasagna

Home

Description

A classic Italian dish layered with pasta, cheese, and rich meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles as per package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by browning the beef and adding tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
