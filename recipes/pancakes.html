Pancakes

Home

Description

Fluffy, golden-brown pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch, topped with syrup and your favorite fruits.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side until golden.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, fruits, or whipped cream.
