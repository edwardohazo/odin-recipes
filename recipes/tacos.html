Tacos

Home

Description

A delicious and easy-to-make taco recipe with fresh ingredients and bold flavors.

Ingredients

  • 12 taco shells
  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet until fully cooked.
  2. Warm the taco shells in the oven.
  3. Fill each shell with meat, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  4. Add sour cream and salsa on top.
  5. Serve and enjoy your tacos!
